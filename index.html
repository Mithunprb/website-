 DINO-v2-based Method for Video Anomaly Detection

A DINO-v2-based approach for video anomaly detection, using latent feature extraction and dimensionality reduction with VAE, ICA, and PCA. Anomalies are scored with One-Class SVM or CNN, with ICA, PCA, and OC-CNN included for ablation studies.
